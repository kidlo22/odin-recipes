BLT Recipe

Description

The BLT is one of my favorite sandwiches and so easy to make, I think it goes great with an ice cold tea on a hot summer day

This BLT is super easy to make and will lift your spirits if your having a bad day

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Sourdough Bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayo

Recipe

  1. Take the bread and spead about half a tablespoon of may on it
  2. Add your lettuce
  3. Add your tomato
  4. Add your bacon
  5. Place the other slice of bread on top and enjoy!
