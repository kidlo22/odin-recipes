Cheeseburger Recipe

Description

What can I say about the cheeseburger that has not already been said, it is a staple of the American diet for better or for worse. However no matter the health benefits or lack there of we can all agree that the cheeseburger is one of the best meals in America, this recipe I created will show you exactly how you can make restraunt quality burgers at home.

Ingredients

  • Potato Bun
  • Burger Patty
  • American Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonaise
  • Ketchup

Recipe

  1. Cut up your tomato and lettuce into slices
  2. Take the burger patty and cook for 3 minutes on each side
  3. Combine mayo and ketchup together to make your burger sauce
  4. Toast the buns
  5. Add the burger sauce to both buns
  6. Add the lettuce and tomato to the bottom bun
  7. Add the cooked burger patty to on top of the tomato
  8. Add cheese on the top of the burger and enjoy :)
