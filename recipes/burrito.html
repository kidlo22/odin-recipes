Burrito Recipe

Description

The burrito is one of my favorite foods to eat when im in a hurry just cause its so simple to make and tastes incredible, with this recipe you will be able to make the best burritos for with very minimal effort

You can make a burrito so many different ways however with this recipe im gonna stick with the classic bean and cheese burrito

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • Refried beans
  • Shredded chedder cheese

Recipe

  1. Place the refried beans in the center of the tortilla
  2. Add the shredded cheese on top of the beans
  3. Wrap the tortilla into a burrito and place on the stove to heat both sides
  4. Enjoy!
